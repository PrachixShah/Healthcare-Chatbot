Responsible AI

Microsoft has identified 6 core principles, July 28, 2023

Microsoft has identified six principles that we believe should guide AI development and use:

  • Fairness: AI systems should treat all people fairly
  • Reliability/Safety: AI systems should perform reliably and safely
  • Privacy and security: AI systems should be secure and respect privacy
  • Inclusiveness: AI systems should empower everyone and engage people
  • Transparency: AI systems should be understandable
  • Accountability: People should be accountable for AI systems

AI and Ethics in Medicine

Challenges and Opportunities, August 15, 2023

AI has the potential to revolutionize medicine, but it also raises important ethical questions. One of the main challenges is ensuring patient privacy and data security. Additionally, there are concerns about bias in AI algorithms and the potential for automation to replace human doctors. However, AI also offers opportunities for more personalized and efficient healthcare.
